About

Coffee is the most popular drink worldwide with more than two billion cups consumed every day. Café culture has continued to boom in Ireland, a large percentage of people who visit coffee shops do so at least once a week, whilst some of us visit on a daily basis. Providing some useful information about coffee, we hope to serve the greater coffee community with this Website.

Health

No doubt about it, coffee is a hot topic. It has become one of the most heavily researched commodities in the world and new data on coffee and health continues to get published frequently.
